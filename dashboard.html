About

TechWorks was created to allow engineering technicians and project managers the ability to prepare for industry related certifications quickly and efficiently. Engineering technicians are able to use the training modules to prepare for certifications and proficiency exams. Technicians are even able to take practice examinations that immolate test like questions and get them prepared.

Help your technicians pass thier material testing and inspection certifications here with practice exams and training materials. There are also tools that can keep a technician or Project Manager updated with industry conditions, whether its local news or weather with in the area.

The content on this page is made to help technicians pass the following certifications:

  • WACEL
  • ACI
  • VDOT
